# Concept Notes - 016 Electric Folk D Major

## Target
Fast electric folk. Forward motion from first bar to last. No breakdown, no ballad bridge.

## What to listen for
- Acoustic guitar must feel like engine.
- Snare backbeat must stay dominant.
- Fiddle should feel like hook instrument, not background pad.
- Electric guitar should answer/extend fiddle and vocal.
- Bass should lock to kick and keep danceable pulse.

## Production questions for v2
1. Upright bass or electric bass? Electric gives harder rock drive; upright gives folk identity.
2. Vocal melody: keep guide contour or write lyric-driven phrasing?
3. Fiddle density: current reel energy may need space under vocal.
4. Drum kit: keep straight rock backbeat or add folk percussion layers.
