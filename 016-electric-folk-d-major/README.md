# 016 - Electric Folk D Major

## Concept
Energetic electric folk band sketch at 130 BPM in D major. Driving acoustic rhythm guitar supports electric melodic guitar, prominent fiddle, bass, live drums, and one clear lead vocal guide. No slow or introspective sections.

## Key & Tempo
- Key: D major
- Tempo: 130 BPM
- Meter: 4/4
- Length: 32 bars, about 71.7 seconds

## Instrumentation
- Acoustic rhythm guitar: boom-chuck 8th-note drive
- Electric guitar: melodic hook lines
- Fiddle: prominent reel-like 16th-note runs
- Bass: electric bass, root/fifth drive
- Drums: live kit, strong backbeat on 2 and 4
- Vocal: single lead guide line, clear/powerful contour
