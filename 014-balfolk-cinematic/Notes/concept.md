# Project 014: Balfolk-Cinematic Hybrid

## Concept
A transition piece moving from a communal **Balfolk Jig (6/8)** into a grand **Cinematic Epic** finale.

## Instruments
- **Balfolk Section**: Violin (40), Accordion (21).
- **Cinematic Section**: Epic Strings (48), Tuba/Bass (58), Timpani (47).

## Structure (32 Bars)
- **Measures 1-8**: Traditional Jig (Violin/Accordion).
- **Measures 9-16**: Thematic expansion (Strings entering, low pedal notes).
- **Measures 17-24**: Build (Spiccato ostinatos alongside Jig melody).
- **Measures 25-30**: Peak (Full ensemble, massive reverb).
- **Measures 31-32**: Decay / Final C-Dorian pedal.
