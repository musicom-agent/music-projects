## Metrical lesson

- Backbeat remains straight: snare on 2 and 4.
- Subdivision swings: long-short triplet eighths.
- Blues rock power comes from stable backbeat + unstable blue notes.
- The b7 in guitar riff creates dominant/blues color without full dominant chord spelling.
