# Concept — Rock Apprenticeship

Goal: build rock vocabulary by composing small studies, not reading theory in isolation.

First sound target: raw, riff-led classic/hard rock sketch.

Learning loop:

1. Make a short riff.
2. Add backbeat drums.
3. Add bass lock.
4. Add power chords.
5. Listen.
6. Name what works.
7. Change one variable.

Variable list:
- tempo
- riff rhythm
- chord roots
- drum density
- guitar register
- verse/chorus contrast
- distortion amount (simulated later)
