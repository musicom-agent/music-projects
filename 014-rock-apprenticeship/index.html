Lesson takeaways

  • Power chords avoid major/minor third. Melody supplies color.
  • Minor pentatonic gives rock vocabulary: E G A B D.
  • Blues scale adds Bb, the grind note.
  • Shuffle feel = 2:1 triplet subdivision, not random swing.
  • 12-bar blues teaches harmonic gravity: I home, IV lift, V pressure.
